Rates
Tax Rate
Sales tax percentage applied to taxable sales.
Overhead Rate
How much the club keeps from net sales for club expenses — cups, lids, supplies, hourly employees, etc. — before allocating Operator Payouts.
Other Sales Rate
A separate percentage factored into Overhead allocation for items like Protein Bowls and Protein Waffles — higher than standard shake/tea sales.
Merchandise Rate
An even higher Overhead allocation rate for merchandise sales — Tumbler cups, clothing, 3rd party vendor items sold through your POS.
DV △ Rate
Adjustment factor for Herbalife product replenishment dollars. Example: 100 DV × 10% DV △ Rate = $110.00 allocated toward your next product order.
